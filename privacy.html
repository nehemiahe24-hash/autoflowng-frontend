Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 17, 2026  ·  Effective immediately

AutoFlowNG ("we", "us", or "our") operates the AutoFlowNG business automation platform at autoflowng-frontend.vercel.app. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights as a user.

By creating an account or using our platform, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account data: Your name, email address, and password (stored as a secure hash — never plain text)
  • Business data: Business name, referral code, and subscription plan
  • Usage data: Platform connections you enable, automations you configure, and actions performed
  • Payment data: Transaction references and plan details — card information is processed entirely by Paystack and never stored by us
  • Google account data: If you connect Gmail, we access only the specific permissions you explicitly grant, limited to what your automations require
  • AI conversation data: Messages sent to our AI Agent and Knowledge Hub
  • Technical data: Session cookies, browser type, and timezone (used only for regional pricing detection)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your AutoFlowNG account
  • To run the business automations you configure
  • To process payments and manage your subscription via Paystack
  • To send transactional emails — account verification, payment receipts, trial reminders
  • To power AI Agent and Knowledge Hub features via the Anthropic Claude API
  • To calculate and pay referral earnings
  • To detect fraud and ensure platform security
  • To improve our platform based on aggregated usage patterns

3. Google API — Limited Use Policy

AutoFlowNG's use of information received from Google APIs strictly adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

  • We only request Gmail permissions strictly necessary for automations you explicitly enable
  • We do not sell, transfer, or use Google user data for advertising or any purpose unrelated to your configured automations
  • We do not allow humans to read your Gmail data except when required for security investigation or to comply with applicable law
  • You can revoke Gmail access at any time via your Google Account permissions page

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with these trusted service providers:

  • Paystack — payment processing (paystack.com)
  • Anthropic — AI features (anthropic.com)
  • Resend — transactional email delivery (resend.com)
  • Supabase — secure database storage (supabase.com)
  • Render — backend hosting (render.com)
  • Vercel — frontend hosting (vercel.com)

5. Data Security

  • All data is transmitted over HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • Passwords are hashed using bcrypt — never stored in plain text
  • Payment card details are never stored by AutoFlowNG — handled entirely by Paystack's PCI-compliant infrastructure
  • Session tokens are stored in secure, HTTP-only cookies

6. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. Upon account deletion, we remove your personal data within 30 days, except where required by law to retain it longer.

7. Your Rights

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Disconnect any connected platform at any time from your dashboard
  • Withdraw Google permissions at any time via myaccount.google.com/permissions
  • Request an export of your data by contacting us

8. Cookies

We use a single session cookie (af.sid) to keep you logged in. We do not use advertising cookies, tracking pixels, or third-party analytics cookies.

9. Children's Privacy

AutoFlowNG is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us personal information, contact us immediately and we will delete it.

10. International Users

AutoFlowNG is operated from Nigeria. By using our platform, you consent to the processing of your data in Nigeria and on servers operated by our hosting providers (Vercel, Render, Supabase).

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes via email or a prominent notice on the platform. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 20, 2026  ·  Effective immediately

Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains what data AutoFlowNG collects, why we collect it, and how we protect it. We will never sell your personal data to third parties.

1. Who We Are

AutoFlow is a global business automation platform that helps businesses and individuals worldwide connect and automate their social media and messaging platforms. This Privacy Policy applies to all users of our website and services at autoflowng.com.

2. Data We Collect

Data Type What We Collect Why
Account Info Name, email address, password (hashed with bcrypt) To create and manage your account
Payment Info Email and transaction reference (via Paystack) To process subscription payments
Platform Tokens OAuth access tokens for connected platforms To perform automations on your behalf
Usage Data Actions run, automations created, platforms connected To provide the service and enforce plan limits
Referral Data Referral links clicked, referral earnings To credit your referral balance
Technical Data IP address, browser type, device info For security and abuse prevention

We do NOT collect or store the content of your messages, posts, or communications sent through connected platforms beyond what is necessary to execute your automations.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide, operate, and improve the AutoFlowNG platform
  • To process payments and manage your subscription
  • To execute automations you configure on connected platforms
  • To send you important account notifications and service updates
  • To detect and prevent fraud, abuse, or security threats
  • To calculate and pay out referral earnings

We will only send marketing emails if you have opted in. You can unsubscribe at any time.

4. Third-Party Platform Connections

When you connect a platform (e.g. WhatsApp, Gmail, TikTok), we receive an OAuth access token from that platform. This token is:

  • Stored securely in our database with access controls in place
  • Used only to perform the automations you configure
  • Never shared with other users or third parties
  • Revocable at any time by disconnecting the platform in your dashboard or revoking access in the third-party platform's settings

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data. We may share data only in these limited cases:

  • Paystack — for payment processing (subject to Paystack's privacy policy)
  • Render / Vercel — our hosting providers, who store data on secure servers
  • Legal obligations — if required by applicable law or a valid court order in your jurisdiction

6. Data Security

We implement security measures to protect your data:

  • All data transmitted over HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • Passwords hashed using bcrypt (never stored in plain text)
  • Database access restricted to authorized backend services only
  • Admin endpoints protected by secret key authentication
  • Rate limiting applied on authentication endpoints to prevent brute-force attacks

While we take every reasonable precaution, no system is 100% secure. Please use a strong password and keep your account credentials private.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Disconnect any connected platform at any time
  • Opt out of marketing communications
  • Export your data in a portable format

Users in the European Economic Area (EEA) and UK have additional rights under GDPR, including the right to object to processing and to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at support@autoflowng.com. We will respond within 7 business days.

8. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account:

  • Your personal data is permanently deleted within 30 days
  • OAuth tokens are immediately revoked and deleted
  • Payment records may be retained for up to 7 years as required by applicable financial regulations in your jurisdiction

9. Cookies

AutoFlowNG uses essential cookies to keep you logged in and maintain your session. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but this may affect platform functionality.

10. Children's Privacy

AutoFlowNG is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If you believe a minor has created an account, please contact us immediately.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via email or an in-app notice at least 7 days before the changes take effect. Your continued use of AutoFlowNG after changes means you accept the updated policy.

Privacy concerns or questions?

Contact our Data Protection Officer directly. We take privacy seriously and respond within 7 business days.
